Looking to join a Golf Society

If you're into golf and looking to join a society then you have come to the right place. Here at Firhouse Golf Society we are always looking for new members to sign up and involed in our many outings per year. We will also be doing one outing per year abroud in sunny Spain or Portugal. The more people we can get for our outings the better deal we can get, so it is a good insentive to come along as much as possible. We will release the up coming outings a few months in advance so people can plan ahead. So if you're looking to get involed don't hesitate to get in touch.

Benefits of Joining a Golf Society

Joining a golf society can offer many benefits to golfers. It provides an opportunity to play with new people who share a passion for the sport, develop new friendships, and most importantly improve your game. Societies often organize regular games and tournaments, which provide an opportunity to compete against others, learn new skills, and have fun. Societies also offer opportunities to travel to new courses and locations, experience new challenges, and make lasting memories.

About FHGS

Firhouse Golf Society was founded in March 2023 for the purpose of playing golf all over Ireland and abroad with with friends. The society has 20 members and are looking for new members all the time who would be intrested in playing some of the best courses in Ireland. We will be doing a minimum of 10 outings per year and will also be two competitions open to all memebers. The Captains Prize, and the Presidents cup. We will also have a golfer of the year award for the player who has been the most consistent player throughout the year.
